## Flagship Projects

### Presence Pro — *AI-Powered Attendance Management*

The most advanced university attendance system:
- **5 verification modes**: QR Code, NFC, Facial Recognition, Geolocation, Manual
- **Real-time analytics** with Excel/PDF exports & Google Drive backup
- **6 languages**: French, English, Arabic, Spanish, German, Chinese
- **Integrated payments**: Chargily (Algeria) + Stripe (International)
- **Military-grade security**: AES-256 encryption, biometric auth
- **Cloud-first**: Firebase Firestore with offline capabilities

### Tutorat Platform — *Smart Academic Tutoring*

Official tutoring management system for Université Oran1:
- **Smart group matching**: AI-assisted tutor-student pairing
- **Hybrid scheduling**: In-person & video conference sessions
- **Department dashboards**: Statistics by faculty & department

[naribi.github.io/tutorat-platform](https://naribi.github.io/tutorat-platform/)

### SiteGuard — *Construction Site Attendance*

Specialized workforce attendance for construction & field operations:
- **GPS geofencing**: Auto check-in/out by location
- **NFC badge scanning**: Rugged environment support
- **Time tracking**: Hours worked, overtime, shift management
